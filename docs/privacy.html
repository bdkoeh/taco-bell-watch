Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Overview

Tbell Compass ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe in transparency about how your data is used.

Location Data

The App uses your device's location and compass heading to find the nearest Taco Bell and point you toward it. Your location is used in real time on your device and sent to Apple's MapKit service to search for nearby Taco Bell locations.

Your location is never stored, collected, or transmitted to any server we operate. We do not have servers. The App runs entirely on your Apple Watch.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Specifically:

• No analytics or tracking
• No user accounts
• No cookies
• No advertising
• No data shared with third parties (other than Apple's MapKit for location search)

Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple MapKit to search for nearby Taco Bell locations. This is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
