Requirements

Tbell Compass requires an Apple Watch Series 5 or later running watchOS 10 or newer. The compass heading feature requires a magnetometer, which is available on Series 5 and all newer models.

Troubleshooting

The compass isn't moving

Make sure you've granted location permissions. Open Settings on your Apple Watch, go to Privacy & Security → Location Services, and ensure Tbell Compass is set to "While Using."

No Taco Bell found

The app searches within approximately 10 miles. If you're in an area without a nearby Taco Bell, you'll see an error message. Try again when you're closer to civilization.

The direction seems off

Magnetic interference (metal objects, electronics) can affect the compass. Try moving away from large metal objects and recalibrate by rotating your wrist in a figure-eight motion.
